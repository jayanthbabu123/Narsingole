Narisingolu is a Village in Zarugumilli Mandal in Prakasam District of Andhra Pradesh State, India. It belongs to Andhra region

It is located 32 KM towards South from District head quarters Ongole. 7 KM from Zarugumalli. 321 KM from State capital Hyderabad
